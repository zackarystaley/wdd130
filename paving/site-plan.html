Purpose

To help those who need a quote on how much it would cost to pave their driveway with asphalt or any area they want paved. This website would also give them a time estimate of how long it would take for them to have their driveway paved.

This website if for anyone who wants to have their driveway paved with asphalt but they do not know how much it would cost. More specifically people who know what they want but they do not know how to get what they want because of the challenge to get information.
